About Me

Hello! I’m Derrick, a passionate and detail-oriented Software Engineer with 3+ years of experience building scalable web applications and cloud-native solutions. My journey in tech started when I wrote my first line of Python to automate a tedious task—since then, I’ve been obsessed with writing clean, efficient code that solves real-world problems. I specialize in: ✔ Backend Development (Node.js, Python, Go) When I’m not coding, I’m either: :books: Reading tech blogs (or sci-fi novels), :headphones: Listening to Playboi carti , :seedling: Contributing to open-source projects, :basketball: Playing basketball to clear my mind. I believe in: "Code should be like a good story—clear, maintainable, and impactful."
